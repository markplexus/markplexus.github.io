FX Package Options

Packages are customised to suit each business needs.

Spot Transfer

Make a one-off transfer on the spot. Your currency will be bought or sold using the current exchange rate and transferred.

Forward Contract

Fix a current rate for transactions up to two years in advance and draw from the agreed amount to make transactions throughout the period.

Limit 'Market' Order

Agree a 'target' exchange rate that you want to exchange at and your broker will process your transaction once the rate is achieved.

Stop Loss

Limit your loss if you’re aiming for a higher rate and the market takes a downturn, by putting a Stop Loss in place.
